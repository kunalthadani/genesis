Genesis is a three day long event comprising of workshops conducted by experienced industry people as well as accomplished seniors in the college targeted towards the members of IECSE, the official computer science club of MIT Manipal. The aim of this event is to introduce various fields of computer science to students of all fields and hence uplift the technical culture of the college.
